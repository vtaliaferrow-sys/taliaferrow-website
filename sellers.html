For Sellers

Selling on your terms.

Life transitions require more than marketing and negotiation. They require structure, coordination, and the ability to guide people through consequential change with clarity and care.

Begin a Conversation

For many sellers, a home is more than a financial asset.

It may represent decades of ownership, the settlement of an estate, a family transition, a relocation, a change in health, or the quiet recognition that one chapter is ending and another is beginning.

The Taliaferrow Team represents sellers throughout Westchester County and New York City with a calm, strategic approach to pricing, presentation, timing, negotiation, and privacy.

How We Work

A different kind of representation.

Some properties benefit from broad visibility. Others require restraint.

We prepare every property intentionally, through pricing strategy, presentation, photography, staging guidance, buyer positioning, and negotiation calibrated to the realities of both the market and the moment surrounding the sale.

In more sensitive situations, discretion matters just as much as exposure.

The Standard No pressure to move faster than the circumstances require. Full visibility when it serves the sale, restraint when the situation calls for it. What is private stays private, at every step.
Experience

Experience with complex sales.

The Taliaferrow Team works frequently alongside:

  • Adult children coordinating family decisions
  • Long-term homeowners navigating significant transitions
  • Estate and probate attorneys
  • Matrimonial attorneys
  • Fiduciaries and trustees
  • Financial advisors and CPAs

Because in many transactions, real estate is rarely the only thing happening, and almost never the most difficult part.
